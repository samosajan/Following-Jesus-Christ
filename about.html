About Us

Welcome to Following Jesus Christ, a platform dedicated to spreading the teachings of Jesus Christ and helping people grow in their faith.

Our mission is to provide easy access to the Bible, worship songs, live sermons, and resources that inspire and uplift your spiritual journey.

We believe in the power of God's word and the importance of community in following Jesus. Join us as we walk together in faith, hope, and love.
